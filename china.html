China

Those places are in the different provinces in China.

Shanghai Wild Animal Park is one of the first AAAA national visiting spots. More than 200 kinds representative and rare animals form the world were collected in SWAP, and the number of animal is about 10,000, such as foreign giraffe, zebra, antilope white rhinoceros and so on, at the same time such as national prior protected giant panda, snub-nosed monkey, Chinese tiger.

Yellow Crane Tower, one of Four Major Towers in China, is situated at the top of Snake Hill, Wuchang City, to the south of Changjiang Bridge. Yellow Crane Tower is 51.4 meters high with five storeys. It consists of the main tower, Pei Pavilion, veranda with windows, memorial archway, Nanlou Tower, and veranda with poem steles. Scholars in the past dynasties have written many immortal poems here.

The Bund, also called Waitan, is a famous waterfront on the west bank of Huangpu River and regarded as the symbol of Shanghai. Here, the charm of Shanghai as a bustling metropolis combining the century-old history and flourishing future is fully presented, making the Bund Shanghai a must-see attraction.

Wuhan Haichang Polar Ocean World is a large multistory combined-type indoor polar ocean theme park in China. It integrates tourism, business and residence with a total investment of 3 billion yuan and construction area of 1.06 million square meters, including polar ocean world park of 4,000 square meters. By the exhibition and performance of oceanic wild and precious animals, such as emperor penguins, polar bears, white whales, walrus and dolphins, Wuhan Haichang Polar Ocean World provides a window of looking into the ocean and experiencing style and features of south and north poles.
